Our Portfolio

Selected projects showcasing our expertise in structural design, supervision, and execution across Ghana.

University of Ghana Business School Lattice Roof Design

Structural design support and on-site supervision for ~50m × 20m all-steel lattice roof canopy featuring integrated light funnels and custom steel tree branch arms. Ensured buildability, quality control, and safe installation.

Love Economy Church Extension - Phase 2

Standalone two-storey steel-framed building (~28.1m x 13.0m footprint) for multi-use (teens ministry & lounge/office). Portal frame with composite slab floors, connected via steel bridges, and two mechanized roof pergolas.

Nyaho Clinic Extension

~1,200 sqm composite steel mezzanine and multi-level extension to existing health-sector facility using locally fabricated steel. Optimized for phased execution to maintain operations while expanding capacity and workflow.
